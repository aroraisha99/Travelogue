Delve In The beauty Of Jahangir Mahal

Beautifully built Palace for Akbar’s wife Jahangir Mahal, is one of the remarkable places in Agra. Showcasing elegant Islamic architecture, one can take a glance at the exceptional artistry. The major source of attraction of this palace, is the huge bowl carved out of a rock, called the “Hauz-i-Jahangiri”, which was used as the container for fragrant rose water.

Witness The Historically Rich- Itimad-Ud-Daulah

Built by Nur Jahan, wife of the Mughal Emperor Jahangir, this magnificent edifice is located on the eastern bank of Yamuna. Established in memory of her father Mirza Ghiyas Beg, who was titled as the Itimad-Ud-Daulah, which means “Pillar of the Government. Commonly known as the Baby Taj as it resembles the Iconic “Taj Mahal”.
